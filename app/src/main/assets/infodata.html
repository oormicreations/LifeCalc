Life Calculator

This calculator computes the time one spends on daily activities. The Available time per day number is the time you are left with at the end of a day to pursue your personal goals.

The Time available in remaining life is the number of years left in your life to achieve those goals, after deducting all the time that you will spend in other activities for the remaining of your life. The remaining life time is the difference of an optimistic effective total life time (90 years) and current age.

The total available time is for your goals only, no sleep, no food, and no other activities whatsoever. Note that you may retire from work/job after 60, so you should enter an average value for work hours if you are younger than 60.

The point is to estimate how much of our precious life is wasted in mere survival, and how insignificant is the amount of time we devote for higher purposes, such as Spiritual Practices. Ideally, a seeker tries to reverse the situation.

This app is open source and free. Please share :)
